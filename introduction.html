JJ LIN

Born 27-3-1981
Citizenship Singapore
Alma mater St. Andrew's Junior College
Occupation
  • Musician
  • Singer
  • Songwriter
  • Actor
  • Film Director
Years active 2003–present

JJ Lin was born in Singapore on March 27, 1981. His ancestral home is Tongan District, Xiamen City, Fujian Province, China. He is a male Chinese pop singer, composer, music producer, and fashion brand manager.

In 2003, he released his first creation album "Le Walker". In 2004, he won the title song "Jiangnan" from the album "Second Heaven" and received widespread attention.

In 2014, his album "Being Because of You" won the 25th Taiwan Golden Melody Award for Best Mandarin Male Singer. In 2016, the album "Dialogue with Myself" won the 27th Taiwan Golden Melody Award for Best Male Singer in Mandarin and Best Composer, and released his first music record "Hear · See Lin Junjie". As of 2020, JJ Lin has released 14 official albums and has gradually composed several songs.

In 2007, JFJProductions, a personal music production company, was established. Founded the trendy brand SMG in 2008. In 2016, he obtained the FIA ​​professional racing license. In 2017, the "SMG" e-sports team was established. In 2004 and 2015, he was invited to sing the theme song of the Singapore National Day Celebration twice. In 2008, he served as the torchbearer of the Beijing Olympic Games. Outside of his acting career, Lin Junjie is enthusiastic about social activities.

In the same year, he composed and sang the song "Love and Hope" for the 5.12 Wenchuan earthquake. In 2010, he served as the torchbearer of the Singapore Youth Olympic Games; in the same year, he composed and sang the theme song "Moving Every Moment" for the Singapore Pavilion at Shanghai World Expo. With his performance in music creation and charity, he won the Singapore Outstanding Youth Award in 2009 and the 5th Business China Youth Award in 2014.



Early life

On March 27, 1981, Lin Junjie was born in a music family in Singapore. Although he has been learning classical piano since the age of 4, he is not strictly speaking a music class, but a background in art. When he was a child, JJ Lin regarded his brother as an idol, and followed his brother's footsteps to do anything. After getting in touch with popular music, he fell in love with the path of creation. In 1996, JJ Lin began to participate in various singing competitions and won several awards for songwriting and singing. In 1999, Haidie Music discovered him in the "very singer training class". This shy and unremarkable high school student showed his innocent passion for music when he played and sang. At that time, Lin Junjie, who had excellent academic performance, gave up his education after receiving the admission notice of the National University of Singapore, and signed a contract with Haidie, thus starting his music path.

Singaporean men are required to perform military service, and JJ Lin is no exception. Because of his expertise, he joined the Singapore Armed Forces Art Corps. During his military service, he accumulated a lot of stage performance experience and also tried countless creations. Although he was repeatedly "shot at guns" in the early days, under the correct guidance and encouragement of the teacher, he was extremely talented and worked very hard, and finally composed a song that was approved by the teacher. This is "Remember" included in the album of Zhang Huimei in 2001. . Since then, Lin Junjie has composed many main songs for Yu Chengqing, Adu, Wang Xinling and others, and his creative talents are finally displayed
